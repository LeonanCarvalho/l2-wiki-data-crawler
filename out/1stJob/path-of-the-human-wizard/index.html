Path of the Human Wizard
Level: 19+
Quest type
Restrictions: None
Race: Human
Class: Human Mystic
Start location Gludin Village / Gludio Territory
Start NPC Parina
Reward 80,314
5,087


Description

  1. Talk to Parina at the entrance to the Temple in Gludin Village.

    Map

  2. Talk to Flame Salamander in the Ruins of Despair.

    Map

  3. Go to the entrance of the Ruins of Despair, hunt Ratman Warrior, until you have Key of Flame

    Map

  4. Talk to Flame Salamander in the Ruins of Despair.

    Map

  5. Talk to Air Sylphid located to the east from Gludin Village.

    Map

  6. Talk to Wasteland Lizardman located not far from Air Sylphid.

    Map

  7. Talk to Air Sylphid located to the east from Gludin Village.

    Map

  8. Talk to Water Undine in Fellmere Harvesting Grounds.

    Map

  9. Hunt Quest Monster Water Seer, until you have Shine Stone  — 2 pcs.

    Map

  10. Talk to Water Undine in Fellmere Harvesting Grounds.

    Map

  11. Talk to Earth Snake in the Ruins of Agony.

    Map

  12. Go down the northwestern road from the Ruins of Agony to hunt Red Bear, until you have Red Soil . Deliver the final blow with a common attack (not with skills) to get the quest item almost immediately.

    Map

  13. Talk to Earth Snake in the Ruins of Agony.

    Map

  14. Talk to Parina at the entrance to the Temple in Gludin Village and get the reward.

    Map

  15. When you get level 20, talk to High Priestess in Einhasad Temple in the Town of Gludio or Gludin Village to transfer your class to Human Wizard.

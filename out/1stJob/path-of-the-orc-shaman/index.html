Path of the Orc Shaman
Level: 19+
Quest type
Restrictions: None
Race: Orc
Class: Orc Mystic
Start location Orc Village / Schuttgart Territory
Start NPC Tataru Zu Hestui
Reward 80,314
5,910



Description

  1. Talk to Tataru Zu Hestui near the House of the King in the Orc Village.

    Map

  2. Collect the items:
    • Go to the Immortal Plateau or to Frozen Waterfalls, hunt Kasha Bear, until you have Kasha Bear Pelt , and Kasha Blade Spider, until you have Kasha Blade Spider Husk .

      Map

  3. Go to the Cave of Trials, hunt Scarlet Salamander, until you have 1st Fiery Egg .

    Map

  4. Talk to Tataru Zu Hestui near the House of the King in the Orc Village.

    Map

  5. Talk to Hestui Totem Spirit near Orc Village.

    Map

  6. Talk to Tataru Zu Hestui near the House of the King in the Orc Village.

    Map

  7. Talk to Priest Umos in Orc Guild in Gludin Village. You can teleport to Gludin Village from Orc Village for free via Gatekeeper Tamil.

    Map

  8. Go to the Fellmere Harvesting Grounds, hunt Grizzly Bear, until you have Grizzly Blood  — 3 pcs.

    Map

  9. Talk to Priest Umos in Orc Guild in Gludin Village.

    Map

  10. Talk to Duda-Mara Totem Spirit near Gludin Village.

    Map

  11. Go to Fellmere Harvesting Grounds, hunt Giant Venomous Spider and Arachnid Tracker, until you have Durka Parasite  — 5-7 pcs. Quest Monster Durka Spirit will appear. Defeat it to get Durka Spirit .

    Map

  12. Talk to Duda-Mara Totem Spirit near Orc Village.

    Map

  13. Talk to Priest Umos in Orc Guild in Gludin Village and get the reward.

    Map

  14. When you achieve level 20, talk to High Prefect Osborn in Orc Guild in Gludin Village or to High Prefect Drikus in Orc Guild in the Town of Gludio to transfer your class to Orc Shaman.

    Map

    1. Go to Ivory Tower Crater, hunt Manashen Gargoyle, collect Manashen's Horn .

      Map

      Map

    Map

  13. Talk to Sagittarius Hamil near Floran Village.

    Map

  14. Go to the Plains of the Lizardmen, hunt Leto Lizardman, Leto Lizardman Archer, Leto Lizardman Soldier, Leto Lizardman Warrior, Leto Lizardman Shaman, Leto Lizardman Overlord, until Quest monster Evil Spirit Kadesh appears (it's important to kill all the types of Leto mobs, otherwise Evil Spirit Kadesh won't appear). Kill Evil Spirit Kadesh with Crescent Moon Bow , don't use any skills.

    Map

  15. Talk to Sagittarius Hamil near Floran Village and get the reward.

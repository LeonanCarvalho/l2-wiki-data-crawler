Test of the Duelist
Level: 39+
Quest type
Restrictions: None
Race: Human, Elf, Dark Elf, Orc
Class: Warrior, Elven Knight, Palus Knight, Monk
Start location Town of Oren / Oren Territory
Start NPC Kaien
Reward 129,600


Description

  1. Talk to Duelist Kaien in Warrior Guild in the Town of Oren.

    Map

  2. Follow the orders of Duelist Kaien:
    • Go to the Wasteland, hunt Puncher, collect Puncher's Shard  — 10 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to Ant Nest, hunt Noble Ant Captain, collect Noble Ant's Feeler  — 10 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to the Cruma Marshlands, hunt Marsh Stakato Drone, collect Drone's Chitin  — 10 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to the Execution Grounds, hunt Corpse Tracker, until you have Corpse Tracker Fang  — 10 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to Breka's Stronghold, hunt Breka Orc Prefect, collect Prefect's Necklace  — 10 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to the Death Pass, kill Fettered Soul and collect Fettered Soul's Chain  — 10 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to Hunters Valley, hunt Tamlin Orc to collect Tamlin Orc's Ring  — 10 pcs., Tamlin Orc Archer, collect Tamlin Orc's Arrow  — 10 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to Ivory Tower Crater, hunt Reinforced Monster Eye, collect Reinforced Monster Eye Meat  — 10 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to the Plains of the Lizardmen, hunt Leto Lizardman Overlord, collect Chief's Amulet  — 10 pcs.

      Map

  3. Talk to Duelist Kaien in Warrior Guild in the Town of Oren.

    Map

  4. Follow the last order:
    • Go to Cruma Tower, on the third floor hunt Krator, collect Krator's Shard  — 3 pcs., and Excuro, collect Excuro's Skin  — 3 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to the Death Pass, kill Grandis and collect Grandis' Skin  — 3 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to Hunters Valley, kill Lakin and collect Lakin's Mace  — 3 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to Timak Outpost, hunt Timak Orc Prefect, until you have Timak Orc's Belt  — 3 pcs.

      Map

  5. Talk to Duelist Kaien in Warrior Guild in the Town of Oren and get the reward.

    Map

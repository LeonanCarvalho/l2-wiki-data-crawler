Test of the Healer
Level: 39+
Quest type
Restrictions: None
Race: Human, Elf
Class: Human Knight, Cleric, Elven Knight, Elven Oracle
Start location Town of Giran / Giran Territory
Start NPC Bandellos
Reward 172,800


Description

  1. Talk to Priest Bandellos in Einhasad Temple in the Town of Giran.

    Map

  2. Talk to Perrin on the eastern hill near Gludin Harbor.

    Map

  3. Quest monster Tatoma will appear. Kill it.

    Map

  4. Talk to Perrin on the eastern hill near Gludin Harbor once again.

    Map

  5. Talk to Allana on the western hill near Gludin Harbor.

    Map

  6. Talk to Father Gupu to the west from the Town of Gludio, you have 2 options:

    Map

  7. Talk to Father Gupu once again and refuse to pay. Then you may skip steps 8-10.
  8. Talk to Father Gupu once again and agree to donate 1,000 adena.

    Map

    • Please note that if you pay 1,000 adena, it'll be added to your quest reward. You will also get a hint where to find Saint Kristin.
  9. Talk to Windy Shaoring near Ant Nest.

    Map

  10. Talk to Father Gupu to the west from the Town of Gludio.

    Map

  11. Talk to Master Sorius in the Warrior Guild in the Town of Gludio.

    Map

  12. Talk to Daurin Hammercrush in Orc Barracks.

    Map

  13. 2 quest monsters will appear: Leto Lizardman Agent and Leto Lizardman Captain. Kill them and get 1st Secret Letter .

    Map

  14. Talk to Daurin Hammercrush in Orc Barracks.

    Map

  15. Talk to the Mysterious Dark Elf at the Altar of Rites.

    Map

  16. 2 quest mobs will appear: Leto Lizardman Assassin. Kill them and get 2nd Secret Letter .

    Map

  17. Talk to the Mysterious Dark Elf at the Altar of Rites.

    Map

  18. 3 quest mobs will appear: Leto Lizardman Sniper. Kill them and get 3rd Secret Letter .

    Map

  19. Talk to Mysterious Dark Elf once again.

    Map

  20. 2 quest monsters will appear: Leto Lizardman Wizard and Leto Lizardman Lord. Kill them and get 4th Secret Letter .

    Map

  21. Talk to Mysterious Dark Elf once again.

    Map

  22. Talk to Saint Kristina near the Altar of Rites.

    Map

    • Please note that if after the dialogue with Saint Kristina the quest does not proceed, go back to the Altar of Rites and talk to Slein Shining Blade. He's located to the left from the Altar.
  23. Talk to Master Sorius in the Warrior Guild in the Town of Gludio.

    Map

  24. Talk to Priest Bandellos in Einhasad Temple of the Town of Giran and get the reward.

    Map

Testimony of Glory
Level: 37+
Quest type
Restrictions: None
Race: Orc
Class: Raider, Monk, Orc Shaman
Start location Town of Giran / Giran Territory
Start NPC Vokian
Reward 188,000



Description

  1. Talk to Prefect Vokian in the Orc Guild of the Town of Giran.

    Map

  2. Prove that you're worth to pass the trial:
    • Go to Ivory Tower Crater, hunt Manashen Gargoyle, collect Manashen Shard  — 10 pcs.

      Map

  3. Go to the Death Pass, kill Guardian Basilisk and collect Guardian Basilisk Fang  — 10 pcs.

    Map

  4. Go to the Wasteland, hunt Tyrant and Elder Tyrant, until you have Tyrant's Talons  — 10 pcs..

    Map

  5. Talk to Prefect Vokian in the Orc Guild of the Town of Giran.

    Map

  6. Talk to Gandi Chief Chianta in Orc Guild in the Town of Dion.

    Map

  7. Collect 5 Scepters:
    • Talk to Priest Manakia in the Orc Guild in the Town of Giran.

      Map

    • Talk to Breka Chief Voltar in Breka's Stronghold, 2 monsters will appear: Pashika the Son Of Voltar and Vultus the Son Of Voltar. Destroy them. Talk to Breka Chief Voltar once again.

      Map

    • Talk to Enku Chief Kepra near Hive Fortress, 2 quest mobs Enku Orc Prefect will appear, defeat them. Talk to Enku Chief Kepra once again.

      Map

    • Talk to Prefect Kasman in Orc Guild in Gludin Village.

      Map

    • Talk to Tunath Chief Harak nor far from the Forgotten Temple.

      Map

    • Talk to Turek Chief Burai in Orc Barracks, 2 quest mobs will appear: Makum Bugbear Thug, kill them. Talk to Turek Chief Burai once again.

      Map

    • Talk to Vuku Chief Driko near the Town of Gludio.

      Map

    • Go to the Cruma Marshlands, hunt Marsh Stakato Drone, collect Marsh Stakato Drone Husk  — 30 pcs.

      Map

    • Talk to Vuku Chief Driko near the Town of Gludio.

      Map

  8. Talk to Gandi Chief Chianta in Orc Guild in the Town of Dion.

    Map

  9. Get the orc heads.
    • Go to Hunters Valley, hunt Tamlin Orc, Tamlin Orc Archer, collect Tamlin Orc Head  — 30 pcs.

      Map

  10. Go to the Timak Outpost, hunt any Timak Orcs, until you have Timak Orc Head  — 20 pcs.

    Map

  11. Talk to Gandi Chief Chianta in Orc Guild in the Town of Dion.

    Map

  12. Talk to Priest Tanapi in the Orc Village.

    Map

  13. Go to Immortal Plateau, Northern Region, hunt Ragna Orc Prefect, Ragna Orc Priest. Quest monster Revenant of Tantos Chieftain will appear, kill it until you have Scepter of Tantos .

    Map

  14. Talk to Priest Tanapi in the Orc Village.

    Map

  15. Talk to Pa'agrio Lord Kakai in the House of the King in the Orc Village and get the reward.

    Map

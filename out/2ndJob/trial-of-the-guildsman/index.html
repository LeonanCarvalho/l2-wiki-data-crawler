Trial of the Guildsman
Level: 35+
Quest type
Restrictions: None
Race: Dwarf
Class: Scavenger, Artisan
Start location Town of Giran / Giran Territory
Start NPC Valkon
Reward 137,600



Description

  1. Talk to Warehouse Keeper Valkon in the Warehouse in the Town of Giran and agree to pay 2,000 adena.

    Map

  2. Talk to Blacksmith Altran in the middle of the Talking Island Village.

    Map

  3. Talk to Warehouse Keeper Valkon in the Warehouse of the Town of Giran.

    Map

  4. Go to the Execution Grounds, hunt Mandragora, Mandragora Sapling, Mandragora Blossom, until you have Mandragora Berry .

    Map

  5. Talk to Blacksmith Altran in the middle of the Talking Island Village.

    Map

    • Recipe: Journeyman Ring - add it to your recipe book by double-clicking.
  6. Gather the items required for the rings: Journeyman Deco Beads  — 7 pcs. and Journeyman Gem  — 7 pcs.
  7. Talk to Blacksmith Pinter in the Blacksmith Shop in the Town of Gludio and carry out his request to get Journeyman Deco Beads  — 7 pcs. If your characters is an Artisan - Recipe: Amber Bead - just add it to you recipe book (by double-clicking).

    Map

  8. Go to Ant Nest, hunt Ant and Ant Captain, collect Amber Bead  — 70 pcs.

    Map

    • If your character is an Artisan, you'll get Amber Bead  — 5 pcs. from mobs, you may also get Amber Lump , used for making Amber Bead  — 5 pcs.
    • If your character is a Scavenger, you'll get Amber Bead  — 5 pcs. from mobs, and Amber Bead  — 10 pcs. when using Spoil.
    • Talk to Blacksmith Pinter in the Blacksmith Shop in the Town of Gludio, get Journeyman Deco Beads  — 7 pcs.

      Map

  9. Talk to Warehouse Keeper Norman in the Warehouse of Gludin Village and carry out his request to get Journeyman Gem  — 7 pcs.

    Map

  10. Talk to Blacksmith Duning in the Blacksmith Shop in Hunters Village.

    Map

  11. Go to Breka's Stronghold, hunt Breka Orc, Breka Orc Archer, Breka Orc Warrior, Breka Orc Shaman and Breka Orc Prefect until you have Duning's Key  — 30 pcs.

    Map

  12. Talk to Blacksmith Duning in the Blacksmith Shop in Hunters Village.

    Map

  13. Talk to Warehouse Keeper Norman in the Warehouse in Gludin Village.

    Map

  14. Collect the necessary materials:
    • Go to Execution Grounds, hunt Quest monsters Lesser Granite Golem, until you have Granite Whetstone  — 70 pcs.; Quest monsters Lesser Corpse Tracker, until you have Red Pigment  — 70 pcs.; Quest monsters Lesser Strain and Lesser Ghoul, until you have Grey Bone Powder  — 70 pcs.

      Map

    • Go to the Plains of Dion, hunt Silenos, until you have Braided Yarn  — 70 pcs.

      Map

  15. Talk to Warehouse Keeper Norman in the Warehouse of Gludin Village to get Journeyman Gem  — 7 pcs.

    Map

  16. Craft Journeyman Ring  — 7 pcs., for each ring you need the following:
    • Journeyman Deco Beads  — 1 pcs.
    • Journeyman Gem  — 1 pcs.
    • Steel  — 1 pcs.
    • Varnish  — 10 pcs.
    • Crystal (D-grade)  — 10 pcs.
  17. Talk to Warehouse Keeper Valkon in the Warehouse of the Town of Giran and get the reward.

    Map

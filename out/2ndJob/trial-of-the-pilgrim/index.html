Trial of the Pilgrim
Level: 35+
Quest type
Restrictions: None
Race: Human, Elf, Dark Elf, Orc
Class: Cleric, Elven Oracle, Shillien Oracle, Orc Shaman
Start location Orc Barracks / Gludio Territory
Start NPC Santiago
Reward 133,300


Description

  1. Talk to Hermit Santiago in Orc Barracks. The Hermit wants you to make a journey and visit Tanapi to learn about Pa'agrio; Gauri Twinklerock to learn about Maphr; Priest Primos to learn about Einhasad; and Andelli to learn about Eva and Shilen.

    Map

  2. Talk to Priest Tanapi in the Orc Village.

    Map

  3. Go to the Cave of Trials and talk to Ancestor Martankus.

    Map

  4. Go to the Forgotten Temple, hunt Quest Monster Lava Salamander, until you have Essence of Flame .

    Map

  5. Go back to the Cave of Trials and talk to Ancestor Martankus.

    Map

  6. Talk to Priest Tanapi in the Orc Village.

    Map

  7. Talk to Gauri Twinklerock in the Dwarven Village.

    Map

  8. Talk to Head Priest of the Earth Gerald

    Map

    (You can pay 5000 Adena, or, if you refuse to pay, talk to Wanderer Dorf).

    Map

  9. Talk to Priest Primos in Einhasad Temple in the Town of Giran.

    Map

  10. Talk to High Priest Petron in the Temple of the Talking Island Village.

    Map

  11. Go under the Elven Ruins, hunt Quest Monster Nahir, until you have Hair of Nahir .

    Map

  12. Go back to High Priest Petron in the Temple of the Talking Island Village.

    Map

  13. Talk to Andellia in the Elven Village.

    Map

  14. Go to the waterfall near Black Rock Hill and hunt quest monsters Black Willow, until you have Debris of Willow .

    Map

  15. Talk to Uruha near the waterfall near Black Rock Hill and get the Book of Darkness .

    Map

  16. Go back to Andellia in the Elven Village. Choose to burn the book of darkness.

    Map

  17. Talk to Elder Casian in the middle of the Wasteland and get the Book of Sage .

    Map

  18. Talk to Hermit Santiago in Orc Barracks and get the reward.

    Map
